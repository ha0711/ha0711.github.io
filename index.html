Hassan Asghar

M.S. in Computer Science, COMSATS University, Abbottabad Campus, Pakistan.

Research Interests : Parallel and Distributed Computing, High Performance Computing, Big Data Analytics and Cloud Computing.

Profile

Hassan Asghar obtained his M.S. in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus, Pakistan. He received his B.S. in Computer Science from Shaheed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto Institute of Science & Technology (SZABIST), Islamabad Campus, Pakistan.
