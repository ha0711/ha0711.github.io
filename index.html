Profile

About

Hassan Asghar is a Ph.D. researcher at KENTECH with research interests in high-performance computing, big data analytics, cloud computing, and network and protocol security. He received an M.S. in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus, and a B.S. in Computer Science from SZABIST, Islamabad Campus. His work combines research-driven problem formulation with hands-on systems experience in benchmarking, performance analysis, and security testing.
