Research Interests: HPC, Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Network & Protocol Security.

Academic Profiles:

Email: hasanasghar at naver dot com

Profile

Hassan Asghar holds an M.S. in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad (Abbottabad Campus) and a B.S. in Computer Science from SZABIST, Islamabad Campus. His technical interests include Parallel & Distributed Computing, High-Performance Computing, Big Data Analytics, and Cloud Computing. He combines a research-oriented mindset with hands-on experience in system benchmarking and performance optimization to effectively bridge theory with real-world engineering solutions.
